About This Blog

Hi, I’m Felicia — welcome to my sewing room! I created this blog to document my passion for handmade clothing, share my process, and hopefully inspire others to pick up a needle and thread.

Whether you’re brand new to sewing or a seasoned stitcher,I hope you find inspiration and practical ideas here. From minimalist garments to warm and cozy classics, this space is all about creativity, craftsmanship, and community.
